Functions that may be less intuitive
  • To indicate interchangeability/closeness between two teams, click on the right-left arrow symbol between them.
  • To edit a judge's call, input their name and updated call. Their previous call will be overwritten.
  • To resolve a dissent, click the radio button beside the winning team
  • To cancel the resolution of a dissent, double-click the radio button you've already clicked
